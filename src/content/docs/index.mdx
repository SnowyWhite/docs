Welcome to the new and official IW4x documentation! 

[IW4x](https://github.com/iw4x/iw4x-client) is a client for **Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 2 (2009)** that lets you to play MW2 in a safe manner with support for dedicated servers, modding, and lots of opt-in features!
