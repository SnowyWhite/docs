### Welcome to

<center>
![Banner](../../assets/img/common/iw4x-logo.svg)
</center>

A community-driven client for [Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 2 (2009)](https://store.steampowered.com/app/10180/Call_of_Duty_Modern_Warfare_2_2009/), with new features such as:

- **Enhanced Security** - All known vulnerabilities of the original game are patched
- **Dedicated Servers** - Browse and play on servers hosted by community members
- **Modding Support** - Play custom game modes like Gun Game or Infected (or create your own!)
- **Controller Support** - Use your Xbox, PS4, or other controllers in the game (no extra software required)
- **More Content** - Maps and Weapons from other Call of Duty titles are included right out of the box
- **Bot Warfare** - Play against smart and configurable bots, either online or in private match
